Our Story

Food grown close,
served with care.

Folium started as a small stall at the Parkview Farmers Market. After two years of weekend pop-ups, we opened our doors — a calm space built around light, wood, and honest cooking.

  • Seasonal menus — changed monthly based on what's fresh and local.
  • Single-origin beans — roasted weekly in small batches by Heartland Roasters.
  • No rush — we encourage lingering. Come for an hour, stay for three.
